# Brand guide

## Files

- `brand/restless-logo.png` — primary mark (use on light/dark backgrounds)
- `brand/restless-banner.png` — wide banner for README/social previews

## Quick rules

- **Do not stretch** the logo (keep aspect ratio).
- Keep **clear space** around the mark (at least the height of the inner icon).
- Prefer using the banner at full width in README.
